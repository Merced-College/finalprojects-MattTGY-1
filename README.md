# cpsc39-finalProjects

Shin Megami Tensei Rougelike by: Matthew Briones and Giovanni Hinojosa

This game is played on the terminal and is based on the Shin Megami Tensei series by Atlus. This combines the ideas from specifically the game Shin Megami Tensei 3: Nocturne but making it different with rougelike elements.

Story: Shortly after the starting events of Shin Megami Tensei 3, in which the Conception created, you, a human was changed to a hybrid between human and fiend, a demifiend. You lost many things that you had when you were once human, including your reason. You use what is known as the Labritnth of Amala, a computer like pillar that is connected to the many different multiverses. Once you use it, you realize you are stuck in it, with no way to escape. Luckily a friendly Fiend, known as Pixie puts under you a spell where you can see the exit, Chests, and enemies. How far can you climb until your demise?

Gameplay: use WASD to move Strength: changes amount of damage Vitality: Health boost per stat Luck: Allows you to have a better chance of negotiations
